# ABSTRACT

In recent years, environmental sustainability has become an important concern for consumers and businesses. Most online shopping platforms focus mainly on product price, ratings, and customer reviews, while ignoring the environmental impact of products.

The proposed system, "AI-Powered Sustainable Shopping Assistant", helps consumers make environmentally responsible purchasing decisions by analyzing product information and predicting a Sustainability Score using Machine Learning techniques.

The system utilizes Amazon product data, H&M product sustainability information, and personal carbon footprint behavior datasets. A Random Forest Machine Learning model is trained to evaluate product sustainability based on various features such as ratings, reviews, purchases, discounts, and sustainability tags.

The system recommends eco-friendly alternatives and helps users understand the environmental impact of their purchasing choices. This project contributes to sustainable development by promoting responsible consumption and reducing environmental impact.

#  INTRODUCTION

##  Background

Online shopping has become an essential part of modern life. Consumers purchase thousands of products daily through e-commerce platforms. However, most consumers are unaware of the environmental impact associated with manufacturing, packaging, transportation, and disposal of these products.

Sustainable shopping encourages consumers to choose products that have lower environmental impact and support responsible resource utilization.

Artificial Intelligence can assist consumers by analyzing product information and recommending environmentally friendly alternatives.
